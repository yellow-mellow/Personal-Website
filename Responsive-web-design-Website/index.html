About Me

Three things that I enjoy...

The beach

The beach is a great place to relax and unwind, as well as a great place for activities such as swimming, sunbathing, and beach sports. I love everything about the beach. The way the sand feels under my feet. The sound of the ocean and the smell of the sea. And if I'm on the Kenyan coast, the view of the sunset and traditional dhows at sea is quite a sight.

Technology

Why do I enjoy Tech? Easy. Technology is interesting because it has the ability to improve and simplify many aspects of our daily lives, from communication and entertainment to education and healthcare. It also drives innovation and progress in various industries, leading to new products, services and ways of doing things. Additionally, technology is constantly evolving and advancing, providing new opportunities for exploration and experimentation.

Creative activities

The phrase "jack of all trades, master of none" is a saying that refers to a person who is skilled in many different areas but is not particularly exceptional in any of them. Be it playing musical instruments, hiking, acting, camping, or travelling, I enjoy taking part in activities that exercise the mind body and soul. I am a curious person, so I often try a lot of new things,but I do not dwell on one thing for too long because there will always be something more exciting. You can call me a jack of all trades and a master of none but it's better than a jack and master of one trade. What do you think?
